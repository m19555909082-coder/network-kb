### 📄 Slide 3：任务描述：隔离办公网络广播风暴Task Description: Isolate office network broadcast storm

<div class="bilingual-cn" markdown>

**🇨🇳 中文**

【任务描述】 【Task Description】
王先生所在公司为成立客户服务部，组建办公网，搭建网络内部服务器，共享办公网信息资源。公司楼上是公司的销售部，由于楼上销售部机位不够，因此销售部中有部分员工的计算机，不得不连接在客户服务部办公网交换机端口上。
由于二个部门共享一台交换机办公，为避免办公网中二个部门之间的工作干扰，以及保护客户服务部客户信息资源安全的需要，需要把两个部门计算机分隔开，形成两个互不连通、互相不干扰的安全网络。

</div>

<div class="bilingual-en" markdown>

**🇬🇧 English**

任务描述：隔离办公网络广播风暴Task Description: Isolate office network broadcast storm
【任务描述】 【Task Description】
Mr. Wang's company for the establishment of customer service department, set up an office network, set up a network of internal servers, sharing office network information resources. The upstairs of the company is the sales department of the company. Because the upstairs sales department is not enough, some employees' computers in the sales department have to be connected to the customer Service Department's office network switch port.
Because the two departments share a switch office, in order to avoid the work interference between the two departments in the office network, as well as to protect the needs of customer service department customer information resources security, it is necessary to separate the computers of the two departments to form two disconnected, non-interference with each other's security network.

</div>
