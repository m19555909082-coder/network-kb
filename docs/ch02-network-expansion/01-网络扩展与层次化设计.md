<div class="bilingual-cn" markdown>

**🇨🇳 中文**

### 📄 Slide 4：网络规划基础：Basis of network planning

<div class="bilingual-cn" markdown>

**🇨🇳 中文**

把分散在校园或各办公区域一个又一个小型网络互相连接起来，形成一个更大范围网络。把区域网络中更多网络设备连接起来，就需要引入更先进网络互连设备，提高网络传输速度。在重新规划中等规模网络环境中，使用更多二层交换机，作为部门网络接入设备，把交换速度更快、网络管理功能更先进的三层交换机，作为连接所有区域核心设备.

</div>

<div class="bilingual-en" markdown>

**🇬🇧 English**

网络规划基础：Basis of network planning
Connect one small network after another scattered in the campus or various office areas to form a larger network. To connect more network devices in the regional network, it is necessary to introduce more advanced network interconnection equipment to improve the speed of network transmission. In the re-planning of the medium scale network environment, more layer 2 switches are used as the department network access equipment, and layer 3 switches with faster switching speed and more advanced network management functions are used as the core equipment to connect all areas.

</div>

<div class="bilingual-cn" markdown>

**🇨🇳 中文**

在组建大中型以太网络之前，需要连接很多的交换机、路由器等网络互联设备。大中型以太网络在规划和设计中，普遍采用三层结构模型，以明确每一台设备在以太网中所承担的基本功能，以太网络在规划和设计中三层结构模型，按照网络的功能，将网络首先从逻辑结构上，划分为三个层次，即核心层、汇聚层和接入层。
层次化网络规划设计

</div>

<div class="bilingual-en" markdown>

**🇬🇧 English**

Before the establishment of large and medium Ethernet, it is necessary to connect a lot of switches, routers and other network interconnection equipment. In the planning and design of large and medium Ethernet, the three-layer structure model is generally used to clarify the basic function of each device in the Ethernet. In the planning and design of Ethernet, the three-layer structure model, according to the function of the network, the network is first divided into three levels from the logical structure, that is, the core layer, the convergence layer and the access layer.
Hierarchical network planning and design

</div>
