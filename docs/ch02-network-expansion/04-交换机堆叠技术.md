### 📄 Slide 16：交换机堆叠技术Switch Stacking Technology

<div class="bilingual-cn" markdown>

**🇨🇳 中文**

堆叠技术就是把交换机的背板带宽，通过专用模块聚集在一起，这样堆叠交换机的总背板带宽，就是几台堆叠交换机的背板带宽之和。堆叠将一台以上的交换机组合起来，视同一个工作组共同使用，如图5-9所示。交换机组可视为一个整体的交换机进行管理。

</div>

<div class="bilingual-en" markdown>

**🇬🇧 English**

交换机堆叠技术Switch Stacking Technology
Stacking technology is to put the backplane bandwidth of the switch together through a dedicated module, so that the total backplane bandwidth of the stacked switch is the sum of the backplane bandwidth of several stacked switches. Stacking combines more than one switch for common use as a workgroup, as shown in the figure below. The switching group can be managed as a whole switch.
