### 📄 Slide 34：路由器的配置端口

<div class="bilingual-cn" markdown>

**🇨🇳 中文**

路由器的配置端口
AUX端口的连接方式
通过Modem之类的拨号设备，以远程方式实现对路由器的配置时，就需要采用AUX端口进行。
AUX接口在外观与RJ-45一样，只是里面所对应的电路不同，实现的功能也不同而已。
根据Modem的端口情况不同，来确定通过AUX端口与Modem进行连接，也必须借助于RJ-45-to-DB9或RJ-45-to-DB25的收发器。

</div>

<div class="bilingual-en" markdown>

**🇬🇧 English**

The configuration port of the router
AUX端口的连接方式
The connection mode of  AUX port
The AUX port is used to configure the router remotely through a dial-up device such as a Modem.
The AUX interface is the same as the RJ-45 in appearance, but the corresponding circuit inside is different and the function is also different.
根据Modem的端口情况不同，来确定通过AUX端口与Modem进行连接，也必须借助于RJ-45-to-DB9或RJ-45-to-DB25的收发器。
Depending on the port of the Modem, the transceiver of RJ-45-to-DB9 or RJ-45-to-DB25 must also be used to connect with the Modem through AUX port.

</div>

